Arthurian Poets: A.C. Swinburne

by A.C. Swinburne

"Arthurian Poets: A.C. Swinburne" is a collection of works by Algernon Charles Swinburne, a significant figure in Victorian poetry, known for his passionate and often controversial verse. This book brings together Swinburne’s contributions to the Arthurian legends, showcasing his imaginative retellings of the stories of King Arthur, his knights, and the mystical elements of Camelot. Swinburne’s lyrical style and deep engagement with myth and legend make this collection a must-read for enthusiasts of Arthurian literature and Victorian poetry. The edition highlights Swinburne's unique take on these timeless tales, offering readers a blend of romance, heroism, and tragedy.
