The Bone Trail

by Paul Stewart

The Bone Trail is the third and final installment of the Wyrmeweald trilogy by Paul Stewart and Chris Riddell. Set in a fantastical world where dragons and humans coexist, the story follows Micah and his friends as they navigate a treacherous landscape littered with the bones of fallen wyrms and bandits. As the battle for the weald intensifies, Micah must confront his own fears and make difficult choices to protect the land he loves. With stunning illustrations by Chris Riddell, this epic adventure is perfect for young readers seeking a thrilling and imaginative tale.
